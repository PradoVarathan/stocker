Technical Signals
📉 MACD
25% weight
Moving Average Convergence Divergence measures trend momentum by comparing two EMAs. A bullish crossover (MACD line above signal line) is a buy signal.
✓ Score 10 if MACD > signal & histogram positive; 0 if bearish crossover
Investopedia StockCharts
📊 RSI
20% weight
Relative Strength Index (0–100) measures whether a stock is overbought (>70) or oversold (<30). The sweet spot for a buy is RSI between 40–60 with upward momentum.
✓ Score 8 if 40 ≤ RSI ≤ 65 (momentum without excess); penalize >75
Investopedia Rayner Teo
📦 Volume Surge
20% weight
High volume on up days confirms institutional buying. A price move on 1.5× average volume is far more reliable than a low-volume move.
✓ Score proportional to recent avg volume / 90-day avg volume ratio
Investopedia
📈 MA Crossover
20% weight
When the 10-day EMA crosses above the 20-day EMA, it signals a short-term uptrend. A recent crossover (within 5 days) scores highest.
✓ Score 10 if 10 EMA > 20 EMA & crossed within 5 days; 7 if just bullish
Investopedia Schwab
🎯 Bollinger Bands
15% weight
Price + 2σ band. When price bounces off the lower band and RSI is not overbought, it's a mean-reversion buy. Middle band = 20-day SMA.
✓ Score 10 near lower band (oversold bounce); 5 near middle; 2 near upper
